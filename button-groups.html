Helper Classes

RyzeUI allows you even more flexiblity over the look and layout of your button groups with these helper classes. You can easily center your button groups on the page by using the additional class centered on your <nav> button group.

Centered Demo

  • Button
  • Button
  • Button

HTML

<nav class="button-group centered">

Position your button group to the right side using the pull-right class on your <nav> button group.
